Welcome to the new [luminusos.org](https://luminusos.org)! The site got a full
rebuild: a fresh look, light and dark themes, English and Portuguese, and
dedicated corners for [Aurora Shell](https://aurora.luminusos.org), this blog
and the [wiki](https://wiki.luminusos.org).

## New here?

LuminusOS is an immutable Linux built on
[Fedora bootc](https://docs.fedoraproject.org/en-US/bootc/). The whole OS
ships as a container image, updates apply atomically in the background, and
rolling back is just a reboot. If an update ever misbehaves, you pick the
previous version in the boot menu and you are back exactly where you were.

The **Workstation** edition is available today: a GNOME desktop refined with
Aurora Shell, ready for work, study and everything in between. **Play**,
**Mobile**, **Tablet** and **Cast** are next on the roadmap, already reserved
in our build system.

## What's next

Take the [Workstation ISO](https://github.com/luminusOS/images/releases/latest)
for a spin, poke around the [wiki](https://wiki.luminusos.org), and tell us
what breaks. Every issue, suggestion and pull request makes the project
better.

See you in the next post!
